Medium Well Steak

Description

A well marbled steak cooked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb Steak
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 1 packet adobo seasoning
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 lime

Steps

  1. Marinate in Lime, adobo and salt for 12 hours.
  2. Grill on skillet on med/high heat for 5 to 6 mins.
  3. Flip and grill for an additional minute.
  4. Let sit for 5 mins.
